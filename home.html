Welcome Our Page

Mission Statement: "Empowering Unemployed Medical Professionals to Improve Healthcare in Underserved Communities by bridging the gap to a Healthier South Africa"

Our objectives as DWJSA:

Provide job opportunities for unemployed medical professionals in South Africa. Offer training and upskilling programs to enhance the employability of medical professionals. Partner with public and private healthcare facilities to increase access to healthcare services in underserved communities. Advocate for policy changes to address the root causes of unemployment among medical professionals.
