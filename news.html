What's currently happening in South Africa.

DWJSA is currently working on a program that connects Unemployed medical professional with job oppurtunities in public and private healthcare facilities, as well as in community-based projects.

Advocacy Program:

DWJSA advocates for policy changes to address the root causes of unemployment among medical professionals, such as limited funding for healthcare and restrictive regulations.
