Founded in 2023 DWJSA is a organisation that provides job opportunitirs for unemployed medical professionals in South Africa. We offer training and upskilling programs to enhance the employability of medical professionals. We've parntered up with with public and private healthcare facilities to increase access to healthcare services in underserved communities.

DWJSA offers training and upskilling programs to enhance the skills and knowledge of medical professionals, making them more employable.

Target Areas:

DWJSA mostly focuses rural areas with limited access to healthcare services,townships and informal settlements with high levels of poverty and unemployment including areas with high rates of infectious diseases, such as HIV/AIDS and TB.

Job Placement Program

DWJSA connects unemployed medical professionals with job opportunities in public and private healthcare facilities, as well as in community-based projects.

Community Health Project

DWJSA partners with community-based organizations to provide healthcare services to underserved communities, while also providing job opportunities for medical professionals.
